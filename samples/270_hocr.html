Sales Bulletin # 010

Installations that involve stairs

As you can understand there is a significant Health and Safety risk associated with the movement of machines and in particular where it involves a ï¬‚ight of stairs.

From now on, where an installation requires devices to be handled up or down stairs there will be an additional cost to the deal to pay for a "removalist contractor" to manage the stairs. This will significantly reduce the risk of workplace injury due to heavy and awkward lifting.

Please liaise with the Service Work Controller to arrange for a removal price. It would be an advantage if you are able to take a photograph of the stairs that need to be negotiated during the delivery of new devices or the removal of old devices this will assist the SWC prepare a quote for the deal.

Overall we are trying to keep the cost of delivery to a minimum and the alternative would be to increase the delivery costs across the board for all machines. By charging specifically for sites that include stairs you will be able to pass the charge on to the customer and keep the general cost of delivery for a site that does not have excessive stairs to a minimum.
